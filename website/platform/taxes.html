The Problems with the Income Tax

Why the Federal Income Tax Must Go:

  • Biggest government intrusion into the lives of the American people - $2.6 trillion extracted from us annually
  • Forces every worker to be a bookkeeper, open records to government, explain expenses, and fear conviction for accounting errors
  • Compliance wastes hundreds of billions of dollars that could be used productively in the economy
  • Discourages savings and investment, creating an enormous drag on the U.S. economy
  • Enables massive government overreach - federal agencies and programs that violate our rights

Ted Brown's Tax Plan

Combine massive spending cuts into a single package that includes the largest tax cut in American history - the total repeal of the federal income tax.

The 100.6 million Americans who pay federal income tax would each see an average of $25,844 per person back in their pockets.

Constitutional Government

There are proposals to replace the income tax with a flat tax or a national sales tax. I will work to repeal the income tax, abolish the IRS, and replace them with nothing.

Even without the personal income tax, the federal government would still collect the same total amount that they did 12 years ago – and the government was much too large even then.

The Constitution allows only a limited role for the federal government, such as national defense, the post office and patents and copyrights. By reducing the federal government to its proper level, we could not only do away with the income tax, but the estate tax, capital gains tax and Social Security tax as well.

The Debt Crisis

Not only does the federal government collect too much money, it spends much more than it collects, and budget deficits keep growing each year. The national debt:

  • Doubled under President George W. Bush
  • Doubled again under President Barack Obama
  • Went up $7 trillion under President Donald Trump
  • Has risen $8 trillion since President Joe Biden was elected

It now exceeds $35 trillion - $104,000 for every man, woman, and child in this country. No one seems to care – except Libertarians. Only Libertarians will cut spending, cut the deficit, and stop adding to this insane debt level that is totally unsustainable.

The Bottom Line

Democratic and Republican politicians are always quite willing to raise taxes. Ted Brown will NEVER vote to raise taxes and will work tirelessly to eliminate the income tax entirely.
