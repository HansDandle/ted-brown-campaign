Immigration

Ellis Island-style system with free movement of people

"Give me your tired, your poor, your huddled masses yearning to breathe free, the wretched refuse of your teeming shore. Send these, the homeless, tempest-tost to me, I lift my lamp beside the golden door!"
- Emma Lazarus "The New Colossus" (on the base of the Statue of Liberty)

People all over the world want to come to the United States to escape political repression, economic stagnation, and other kinds of persecution. This has been the case for 400 years, since the first colonists arrived.

Ted Brown's Family Immigration Story

One of my great-grandfathers was a Lithuanian Jew who fled anti-Semitism in Czarist Russia in 1890. Another great-grandfather fled forced military service in Germany in 1904.

The Immigration Crisis Timeline

1924

Democrats and Republicans enacted a system of immigration controls, creating a never-ending immigration crisis driven by varying views on who should be allowed to come here, often tainted by religious, ethnic, or racial prejudice.

1930s

We first saw the cruelty of these laws when European Jews fleeing Nazi tyranny were denied entry to the U.S.

Today

Any time Congress tries to reform the problem they created, they have failed miserably, causing untold suffering and the creation of an immigration police state.

Current System Failures

The Immigration Police State in Texas:

  • Domestic highway checkpoints - violating 4th Amendment protections
  • Warrantless federal trespasses onto ranches and farms
  • Warrantless searches on Greyhound buses
  • Forced deportations and family separations
  • E-Verify requirements that infringe on liberty and privacy of Americans
  • Massive backlogs where legal applications are ignored for years

The Facts About Immigration

Unlike what you hear from right-wing politicians like Senator Ted Cruz:

Immigrants (both "legal" and "illegal") commit far fewer crimes than native-born Americans. They contribute to our economy, not detract from it.

Ted Brown's Immigration Solution

Ellis Island-Style Immigration System:

  • Free movement of people across international borders with economic liberty and free markets
  • Welcome all foreigners after checking for criminal record and communicable diseases
  • Entry at any international port - airports, water ports, land crossings - not just border towns
  • Work from Day One instead of being placed on welfare programs
  • No border wall needed - eliminate the reason people flock to specific border areas
  • End the immigration police state - no more checkpoints, raids, or family separations

America: A Nation of Immigrants

The U.S. is a nation of immigrants, and we need to recognize and embrace our rich history as a melting pot of people from all over the world, instead of falling prey to ill-informed xenophobia that hurts both Americans and immigrants alike.

Currently, it's almost impossible for most people to come to the U.S. legally. Even those who do apply through the current system are faced with massive backlogs where their applications are ignored.

The Solution is Liberty

As with most issues, the solution is to embrace economic liberty and free markets. That means the free movement of people across international borders.
